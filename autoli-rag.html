Overview

This project was a contribution to a startup-oriented AI workflow for AUTOLI. The work focused on building or contributing to a RAG-style system where answers are grounded in structured information rather than generated blindly.

The goal was to support more useful and context-aware responses by retrieving from prepared material and improving consistency in the answer flow.

Important Implemented Ideas

  • Domain-specific retrieval thinking
  • Structured knowledge handling
  • Cached responses
  • Persistence-oriented response design
  • Vector database or persistent database based storage/retrieval flow
  • Reducing repeated work for already-answered queries
  • Improving consistency and response quality through retrieval grounding

Cached Response Feature

One notable feature was the use of cached responses and persistent storage thinking, so repeated or related queries could be handled more efficiently instead of recomputing everything every time.
